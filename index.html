About Skill Linkr

Skill Linkr is a student-focused freelancing platform designed to connect talented students with real-world projects from businesses and startups. Our mission is to bridge the gap between skills and opportunities.

We aim to empower students with practical experience while helping businesses access affordable and quality digital services.
